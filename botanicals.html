The Company

Perspex SA is committed to recycling of any acrylic scrap. To this end it will appoint dedicated recyclers in each province to assist them in this process.
I have been appointed by Perspex SA as its only dedicated recycler of extruded acrylic in the Western Cape. So far I have
collected from the major distributors but am ready to expand this service to all acrylic customers.

Benfits of joining

Wharehouse space is saved through bags provided by us, reusable acrylic wont be throwen
into landfills driving up raw acrylic prices, Certificates are issued for the volume
saved each year and you will make a contribution to saving the enviroment
